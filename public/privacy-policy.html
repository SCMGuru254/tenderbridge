Introduction

SupplyChain_KE ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and mobile application (collectively, the "Platform").

Information We Collect

When you register an account, apply for jobs, or use certain features of our platform, we may collect personal information, such as:

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Professional information including work experience, skills, and education
  • Resume/CV and other job application materials
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Account credentials

Usage Data

We may also collect information that your browser or device sends whenever you visit our Platform, such as:

  • Your device's IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages of our Platform that you visit
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Time spent on those pages
  • Device identifiers
  • Mobile device information

How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Match you with relevant job opportunities
  • Connect employers with suitable candidates
  • Personalize your experience on our platform
  • Send you job alerts and other notifications
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our website and services
  • Monitor usage of our services
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities
